## Google/DeepMind Updates

### Agent Development Kit (ADK)
- **ADK v2.0.0b1 shipped** (PyPI, Apr 21/22, pre-release). First beta of the 2.0 line (after a1/a2/a3 alphas). Major features: `Workflow(BaseNode)` graph orchestration, `NodeRunner` for isolated per-node execution, `DefaultNodeScheduler` with `ctx.run_node()`, explicit `ReAct` loop nodes replacing legacy flows, HITL resume via event reconstruction, lazy scan deduplication, state/artifact delta flushing.
