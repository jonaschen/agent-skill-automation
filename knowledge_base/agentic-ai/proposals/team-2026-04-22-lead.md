## Proposal 1: Quiet-Day Sweep Compression

**Type**: Process refinement (not structural)
**Priority**: P2

### Problem

The next 2-3 days are likely to be quiet (both vendors in extended silence). A full sweep report (1000+ words), analysis (1500+ words), and discussion (2000+ words) for a no-change day wastes ~$2-3 of API cost and produces no actionable output. The directive already says "single-cycle volume" but doesn't address what to do when a cycle produces zero findings.

### Proposed Change

If both vendors have no new releases AND no material announcements in a 48h window: the researcher produces a **minimal sweep only** (~300 words, day-counts + one-sentence per domain confirmation). No analysis. No discussion. The factory queue provides enough work without new research input.

**Trigger for re-expansion**: >2 simultaneous releases, Opus 4.7 patch, critical CVE, or any event requiring cross-cutting analysis.

### Expected Benefit

- ~$2-3/day API cost saving on quiet days
- Researcher session completes faster (10 min vs 45 min)
- Factory queue gets breathing room to clear backlog

### Cost

None. Quiet days produce no actionable findings regardless of sweep depth.

### Risk

Low. The minimal sweep still checks all tracked release endpoints. It just skips analysis/discussion when there's nothing to analyze or discuss. If the researcher discovers something during the minimal sweep, it can produce a full analysis.

## Proposal 2: Factory Queue Clearance Tracking

**Type**: Process refinement (not structural)
**Priority**: P3

### Problem

The factory queue is at 13 items — the largest it's been. At ~3 items/session, this is ~4-5 sessions to clear. If new ADOPT items continue to be generated faster than the factory clears them, the queue grows without bound. We have no metric for queue health.

### Proposed Change

Add a one-line "Factory Queue Status" to each research-lead directive: `Queue: X items (Y carried, Z new). Clearance rate: W items/session. Est. sessions to clear: X/W.` If the clearance rate drops below 2 items/session for 2+ consecutive sessions, or the queue exceeds 20 items, flag for research-lead assessment.

### Expected Benefit

- Early warning on pipeline bottleneck
- Quantified throughput metric for team assessment

### Cost

One line per directive. 30 seconds.

### Risk

None.
